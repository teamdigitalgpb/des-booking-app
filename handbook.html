Part A — Guest Experience

A1. Booking a Room

Guests visit d-econo-space-agus.vercel.app and click Book a Room or any Book button on the homepage. They fill out a single form at booking.html.

Are you a volunteer?NoCheckboxes: JW · A2/A19 — unlocks lower rates when verified

What happens after Submit

1
Form validates all required fields and checks date conflicts against existing bookings and admin-blocked dates.
2
Booking is posted to Make.com webhook → GHL contact created/updated → row inserted in the DES Bookings sheet.
3
Guest is redirected to payment.html to complete payment.

Room Capacity Rules

  • Room D1: up to 4 guests (AC)
  • Room D2: up to 4 guests (Fan) — can only be booked when D1 is occupied or blocked for the same dates
  • D' Whole Space: up to 8 guests — exclusive for 5+ guests (no other bookings alongside)
  • If booking 5+ guests for D1 + D2, the site recommends switching to D' Whole Space
D2 Rule: If a guest tries to book Room D2 and Room D1 has no existing booking for those dates, the form will show a warning and block submission. They must book D1 first or contact the team.

A2. Volunteer Rates (JW & A2/A19)

Volunteer rates are unlocked directly on the booking form. Two types are supported.

JW (Jehovah's Witness) Rate

1
Guest checks "I am a Jehovah's Witness (JW)" on the booking form.
2
A verification panel appears with this week's question: "What is the [first/last] midweek song for the week of [dates]?"
3
Guest enters the song number. If correct, the JW rate is applied and the price updates instantly.
The question and answer are updated every Monday from the Settings tab of the DES Bookings Sheet. See B5 for the weekly update procedure.

A2/A19 Rate

1
Guest checks "I am an A2 / A19 Volunteer".
2
A panel appears asking for their A2/A19 account number (exactly 7 digits, numeric only).
3
If the format is valid, the A2/A19 rate is applied automatically.
The system only verifies the format (7 digits) — not the actual account. The honor system applies. Admin can follow up via the booking record if needed.

Volunteer Rates Modal (on the homepage)

Each room card on the homepage has a "JW or A2/A19? There's a special rate for you →" badge. Clicking it opens a modal showing the full rate table for that room. Guests can verify right there and then be sent to the booking form with their rate pre-applied.

The modal also has a "Give a donation instead →" link for volunteers who want to donate rather than book.

Voucher Path (alternative)

Guests who want their rate confirmed before booking can go to voucher.html via "Volunteer? Get Your Code". This 3-step flow collects their info, verifies their eligibility, and generates a reference code they bring to the booking form.

  • Step 1: Personal info — name, mobile, congregation, municipality, province
  • Step 2: Choose rate type — JW or A2/A19
  • Step 3 (JW): Answer the weekly midweek song question
  • Step 3 (A2/A19): Answer JW question → identify the Bible book → enter account number or verify by email (code is ISA6:8)

On success, they receive a reference code (REF-[timestamp]) and the submission is posted to the admin webhook with tags DES-voucher-pending and DES-JW or DES-A2A19.

A3. Completing Payment

After booking, the guest lands on payment.html. The flow depends on whether they are a regular guest or a volunteer needing discount verification.

For regular guests

1
Payment methods are shown immediately: GCash · Maya · Bank / InstaPay.
2
Guest taps a method → QR code appears showing the exact amount to send.
3
Guest scans QR in their app and sends the payment.
4
Guest sends their payment screenshot via Facebook Messenger to the D' Econo-Space page.
5
Staff confirms receipt, logs the payment in the sheet, and sends the keyholder PIN via Messenger.

For JW/A2/A19 guests (not yet verified)

If they didn't verify on the booking form, a verification step appears first (same JW question or A2/A19 account number) before the payment methods are shown.

Check-in & Check-out times

  • Check-in: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out: 12:00 NN (noon)

A4. Cancellation

Self-service cancellation is available through a link on the booking confirmation page (thankyou-booking.html).

Cancellation window

Deadline: 12 hours before 2:00 PM check-in — i.e., by 2:00 AM of the check-in date. After that, the self-serve option is no longer shown. Guests must contact the team directly for late cancellations.

How to cancel (guest side)

1
Guest opens their confirmation page or the cancel link in their confirmation email.
2
The cancel page (cancel.html) shows their booking summary and a reason dropdown.
3
They select a reason and confirm. Options: Wrong dates · Change of plans · Found other accommodation · Personal emergency · Other.
4
The cancellation is posted to the Make.com webhook with action: 'cancel' and the tag DES-cancelled. The booking row in the sheet is updated.
5
A success page confirms cancellation and offers a link to re-book.
There is currently no automated refund. Staff must handle refunds manually based on the cancellation reason and timing.

A5. Donating

Any guest or visitor can donate at donate.html, reached from multiple homepage links: "Support someone's stay.", "Donate Now" button, footer link, and the "Give a donation instead →" button in the volunteer rates modal.

Two donation paths

Option A — Reserve a Room and Donate

"Whether you're giving what you're able or more than the listed rate — book your room and let your heart lead."

Clicking this sends the guest to booking.html?donation=1 — the normal booking form, tagged as a donation-reservation so the admin sees it in the sheet.

Option B — Give a Gift (no reservation)

For good samaritans who want to sponsor a volunteer's stay without booking for themselves.

1
Guest chooses a payment method: GCash · Maya · Bank / InstaPay · Wise.
2
A QR code appears (or Wise link for international). Guest scans and sends any amount they choose.
3
Guest enters their Name and Mobile number (the only info required — no email, no purpose, no amount asked).
4
They click Done → webhook fires → Thank You screen appears.
Wise opens in a new tab. The guest enters their name/mobile first, then is taken to the Wise payment link. After paying, they return and see the Thank You screen.

B2. Booking Workflow

Every booking moves through these statuses in the sheet:

New → Confirmed → Paid → Checked-in → Checked-out   or   Cancelled / Declined

① New booking arrives — Status = New

1
Check for date conflicts. Scan the sheet for the same Room with overlapping dates in any Confirmed or Paid row. If none → proceed to confirm.
2
Confirm: Click the Status cell → select Confirmed. Send the Confirmation Email (template in B3).
3
If there is a conflict: Set status to Declined. Send the Decline email. (See ④ below.)

② Guest pays

1
Guest sends a payment screenshot via Facebook Messenger.
2
Check: does the amount match the booking total? Does the sender name match?
3
In the booking row, fill in: Amount Paid · Payment Method · Payment Reference No.
4
Set Status → Paid. Send the Payment Received email.
5
Send the guest the keyholder PIN via Messenger so they can check in independently.

③ Check-in day

1
On the check-in date, update Status → Checked-in.
2
If the guest messages about access, confirm their PIN or assist as needed.

④ Check-out day

1
After 12:00 NN on the check-out date, update Status → Checked-out.

⑤ Cancellations

  • Guest-initiated (self-serve): The system automatically posts a cancellation to Make.com and the sheet updates. Verify the row and mark it Cancelled if not already done.
  • Admin-initiated: Update Status → Cancelled and send the guest a message via Messenger or email explaining the reason.
  • Declined (date conflict): Status → Declined. Send the Decline email.

B4. Handling Messenger

Guests reach you through the D' Econo-Space near CBEC Facebook Page inbox. Common messages and how to respond:

What the guest saysWhat you do
"Is [date] available for [room]?"Check the sheet for that room + dates. Reply with yes/no and direct them to book on the website if available.
"I already paid" (with screenshot)Verify amount and sender name. Log in the sheet. Set Status → Paid. Send the keyholder PIN.
"I want to cancel"Ask them to use the cancellation link on their confirmation page, or update the sheet manually and send the Cancellation Acknowledgement email.
"Can I change my dates?"Check the new dates for availability. If free, update the booking row (dates, total price) and re-confirm via email.
"Can I add more guests?"Check the new guest count is within room capacity. Recalculate the total and update the sheet. Re-send Confirmation email with new total.
Special request (early check-in, etc.)Note it in the sheet's Special Requests column. Coordinate with the keyholder. Reply to the guest when confirmed.
When unsure — a double-booking, a payment mismatch, a special situation — don't guess. Say "Let me check and get back to you shortly." Then ask the owner.

B5. Weekly JW Question Update

Every Monday, update the JW verification question so genuine volunteers aren't rejected. This takes under 2 minutes.

1
Find this week's midweek song number — check wol.jw.org → This Week at a Glance → Midweek Meeting program, or get it from the owner.
2
Open the DES - Bookings Google Sheet → click the Settings tab.
3
Update the two cells:
  • JW_QUESTION → e.g. What is the first midweek song for the week of June 16–22?
  • JW_ANSWER → e.g. 18
4
Save (auto-saves in Sheets). The booking site picks it up within a few minutes — no deploy needed.
⚠️ If you skip a Monday update, real JW volunteers who enter the correct current song will be wrongly rejected. Set a recurring Monday reminder.
Part C — Admin Panel

C1. Accessing the Admin Panel

The admin panel is a password-protected page for managing room availability and date blocks.

  • URL: d-econo-space-agus.vercel.app/admin.html
  • Password: 1992

Once authenticated, the session is held in sessionStorage — closing the browser tab logs you out automatically.

C2. Blocking Dates

Use date blocks to prevent guests from booking during maintenance, private events, or periods when the space is unavailable.

Adding a block

1
Open the admin panel → Add Date Block form.
2
Select Room: D1 · D2 · Both rooms.
3
Enter Start Date. Enter End Date (leave blank for a single-day block).
4
Enter an optional Reason (e.g. "Maintenance", "Owner private use").
5
Click Add Block. The admin webhook (action: addBlock) posts to the Apps Script, which writes to the Blocked Dates tab of the Google Sheet.
6
The booking site reads that tab (published as CSV) on every page load — blocks are active within minutes.

Removing a block

The admin panel lists all active blocks. Click Remove next to a block. The webhook fires action: removeBlock and the row is deleted from the Blocked Dates tab.

Block scope rules

Room selectedEffect on booking form
D1D1 only is blocked for those dates
D2D2 only is blocked
BothBoth D1 and D2 are blocked; D' Whole Space also becomes unavailable

C3. Room Availability Toggle

The admin panel shows a live status card for each room. If a room is free, a Mark Unavailable button appears. If a room is currently blocked, a Mark Available button appears to remove today's block.

This is a quick toggle for urgent same-day changes — the full date-range blocking form in C2 is used for planned closures.

D3. Webhooks & Payloads

1. WEBHOOK_BOOKING (Make.com)

Triggered by: Booking form submit (booking.html).
Destination: Make.com → creates/updates GHL contact → inserts row in DES Bookings sheet.

{
  firstName, lastName, name,
  mobile, email,
  room,           // d1 | d2 | whole
  checkin,        // YYYY-MM-DD
  checkout,       // YYYY-MM-DD
  guests,
  discountType,   // regular | jw | a2a19
  referral,
  specialRequests,
  totalPrice,
  nights,
  rateType,       // regular | jw | a2a19
  shareWaitlist,  // 'yes' or ''
  bookingType,    // 'donation-reservation' or ''
  tags            // e.g. 'DES,DES-booked,DES-d1,DES-share-waitlist'
}

2. WEBHOOK_CANCELLATION (Make.com)

Triggered by: Guest submits cancellation form (cancel.html).
Destination: Make.com → updates GHL contact → applies DES-cancelled tag.

{
  action:   'cancel',
  name, email,
  room, checkin, checkout,
  reason,   // Wrong dates | Change of plans | Found other accommodation | Personal emergency | Other
  tags:     'DES,DES-cancelled'
}

3. WEBHOOK_DONATION (Apps Script)

Triggered by: Donation form submit (donate.html / js/donate.js).
Routing: action: 'donation'.

{
  action:        'donation',
  name,
  mobile,
  paymentMethod, // GCash | Maya | Bank / InstaPay | Wise
  room,          // if coming from volunteer rates modal (d1 | d2 | whole | '')
  tags:          'DES,DES-donation'
}

4. WEBHOOK_VOUCHER (Apps Script)

Triggered by: Voucher request form submit (voucher.html).

{
  name, mobile, congregation, municipality, province,
  rateType,      // jw | a2a19
  idNumber,      // 7-digit account (if A2/A19 code path)
  answer,        // JW question answer
  email,
  voucherMethod, // email | code
  refCode,       // 'REF-' + timestamp
  tags           // 'DES,DES-voucher-pending,DES-JW' or 'DES-A2A19'
}

5. WEBHOOK_ADMIN (Apps Script)

Triggered by: Admin panel actions (admin.html) + abandoned booking beacon.

// Add a date block
{ action: 'addBlock',    room, startDate, endDate, reason }

// Remove a date block
{ action: 'removeBlock', room, startDate, endDate }

// Abandoned booking (fires on page unload if form not submitted)
{ action: 'bookingAbandoned', name, mobile, room, checkin, checkout, totalPrice }

6. WEBHOOK_STORY (Apps Script)

Triggered by: Story submission form (stories.html).

{
  action:   'story',
  name, location, period, story, source,
  tags:     'DES,DES-story-pending'
}
Webhooks 3–6 all post to the same Apps Script URL and are routed by the action field. Only WEBHOOK_BOOKING and WEBHOOK_CANCELLATION go to Make.com.
