Why this place exists

Our Story

D' Econo-Space began with a simple question: How can the burden be lightened for those who willingly sacrifice their time, energy, and personal resources in support of theocratic projects?

Frequently, many volunteers travel from different islands and provinces, willingly giving their time, energy, skills, and personal resources to support sacred assignments. Their love, self-sacrifice, and willingness to serve are deeply appreciated.

The vision for D' Econo-Space grew from personal experience. Volunteers regularly traveled from distant islands to participate in volunteer projects. Like many others, each trip involved searching for accommodations, managing expenses, and finding a place close enough to make daily service practical. This experience inspired a simple desire: to provide a simple and affordable place where volunteers could rest without carrying an unnecessary financial burden.

What began as an empty space gradually became a welcoming home near CBEC — a place where volunteers could find rest, fellowship, and encouragement after a day spent in meaningful service. Its purpose has always been simple: to support those who are supporting others.

D' Econo-Space is sustained through volunteer effort. The cleaning, maintenance, and daily operations are cared for by part-time volunteers who support themselves through cleaning services, errands, and other practical work while continuing to share in volunteer service. Their example reminds us that even small acts of kindness and hard work can contribute to something much larger than ourselves.

More than a place to stay, D' Econo-Space reflects a spirit of mutual support and hospitality. It is not a hotel. It is intended to be a home, though not a very big space but conceptualized by volunteers, for volunteers.

It is our hope that everyone who stays here feels welcomed, valued, and appreciated for the sacrifices they make in support of theocratic projects. If, during your stay, you are moved to help in some way — whether by caring for the space, encouraging fellow volunteers, or sharing toward the ongoing upkeep of the space — you become part of the same spirit that made D' Econo-Space possible. Your support and generosity NOW means there will be next volunteers who can stay and we can't tell, you'll still be here in your next volunteer schedule!
